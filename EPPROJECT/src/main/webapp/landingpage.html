Skill Development

It focuses on skills required in the professional world, such as communication skills, problem-solving abilities etc.It may include workshops, seminars, and practical exercises to improve these skills.

Technical Training

In this specialized training in technical skills relevant to specific industries or job roles and teaching technical concepts,technologies,and hands-on practical sessions to develop proficiency in areas like programming, data analysis, graphic design, etc.
